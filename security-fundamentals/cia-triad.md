# CIA Triad

The CIA Triad is a fundamental cybersecurity model.

CIA stands for:

- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

## Confidentiality

Only authorized users should be able to access data.

Examples:
- Passwords
- Medical records
- Bank details

Protection Methods:
- Passwords
- Encryption
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

## Integrity

Data should not be modified without authorization.

Examples:
- Exam marks
- Payroll records
- Financial transactions

Protection Methods:
- Hashing
- Digital Signatures
- Access Controls

## Availability

Systems and data should be available when needed.

Examples:
- Websites
- Online banking
- Email services

Threats:
- DDoS attacks
- Hardware failures
- Power outages

## What I Learned

- Confidentiality protects data from unauthorized access.
- Integrity ensures data remains accurate and unmodified.
- Availability ensures systems remain accessible.
- Many cybersecurity incidents affect one or more parts of the CIA Triad.
